Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Yamac Bezirgan ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our website (yamacbezirgan.com) and any mobile applications or games we publish (collectively, the "Services").

Information Collection and Use

Our Services are designed with your privacy in mind. Depending on the specific application, we may collect minimal, non-personal data to support core functionality such as saving preferences, game progress, or app settings. This data is typically stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers unless explicitly stated within the application.

Some of our Services may operate entirely offline and do not collect or transmit any data whatsoever.

Data We May Collect

Where applicable, our Services may process the following types of non-personal information:

  • App preferences and settings stored locally on your device.
  • Game progress, scores, or saved states stored locally on your device.
  • Basic, anonymized usage or crash data to help us improve the experience.

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., contacting us for support).

Third-Party Services

Some of our Services may use third-party tools (such as app stores, hosting providers, or basic analytics) that operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

Advertising

Some of our applications may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These networks may use anonymized, non-personal information to serve relevant ads. We do not share personally identifiable information with advertisers.

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information processed by our Services. Where data is stored locally on your device, the security of that data is managed by your device and operating system's built-in protections.

Your Choices

You can typically clear locally stored data by clearing your browser or app data through your device settings. Uninstalling an application will remove any locally stored data associated with it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
